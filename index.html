FAQ

Questions, answered

How is this different from a calorie tracker?

Trackers log the past. SnapMeal plans the future: it generates your week of meals first, then the grocery list to make it happen. Tracking becomes unnecessary — you already know what you're eating.

What does the fridge scan actually do?

You photograph your open fridge or pantry. SnapMeal identifies usable ingredients and reworks the week's recipes to use them first — cutting your grocery bill and your food waste.

Does it handle allergies and diets?

Yes — set eating styles (high-protein, vegan, keto, low carb and more) and hard exclusions like nuts, gluten or pork. Excluded ingredients never appear in any plan.

Can I swap a meal I don't like?

One tap. Every swap respects your goal, budget and what's already on your grocery list.

How do I cancel?

Subscriptions run through Google Play or the App Store — cancel there in one tap, keep Pro until the period ends.
